# Product Guide: Savings Accounts

## Standard Savings Account
- Minimum opening deposit: $25
- Annual Percentage Yield (APY): 0.50%
- Monthly maintenance fee: $5 (waived with $300 minimum balance)
- No limit on withdrawals

## Premium Savings Account
- Minimum opening deposit: $1,000
- Annual Percentage Yield (APY): 2.75%
- Monthly maintenance fee: $15 (waived with $10,000 minimum balance)
- Includes complimentary financial planning consultation

## High-Yield Money Market Account
- Minimum opening deposit: $2,500
- Annual Percentage Yield (APY): 3.25%
- Monthly maintenance fee: $25 (waived with $25,000 minimum balance)
- Check-writing privileges included
- Tiered interest rates based on balance

*APY rates are variable and subject to change. Fees may reduce earnings.*

# Product Guide: Checking Accounts

## Basic Checking
- No minimum opening deposit
- No monthly maintenance fee
- Free debit card
- Free online and mobile banking

## Business Checking
- Minimum opening deposit: $100
- Monthly maintenance fee: $15 (waived with $5,000 average balance)

- Free business debit card
- Required documents: Business license, EIN, articles of incorporation

## Premium Checking
- Minimum opening deposit: $500

- Unlimited transactions
- Preferred rates on loans
- Free cashier's checks and wire transfers

# Mortgage Products

## Fixed-Rate Mortgage
- Terms: 15-year, 20-year, 30-year
- Current rates: Subject to market conditions; contact a loan officer for current rates
- Minimum down payment: 3% (with PMI) or 20% (no PMI)
- Closing costs: Typically 2-5% of loan amount

## Home Equity Line of Credit (HELOC)
- Variable rate based on prime rate
- Draw period: 10 years
- Repayment period: 20 years
- Required documents: Proof of income, property appraisal, homeowners insurance, government-issued ID, recent tax returns
- Maximum LTV: 85%

*All loan products are subject to credit approval. Rates and terms may vary based on creditworthiness and market conditions. This is not a commitment to lend.*
